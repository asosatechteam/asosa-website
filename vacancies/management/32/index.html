Senior Project Manager

We are looking for a passioned Project Manager to join our team in Kyiv. We work with some top tier clients like Google or COTY and really looking for a person driven by customer success who has a proven track record of working with relevant context.

You can watch a testimonial from Google here

Required skills

  • 4 years+ experience as Project Manager
  • Strong understanding of the software development life cycle (and be able to explain it to clients)
  • Knowledge of Scrum, Kanban
  • Proactive and self-motivated
  • Find decisions to non-standard tasks
  • Able to hold a literate communication with a client
  • Strong analytical skills
  • Strong negotiation skills
  • English language level should be C1(Advanced)
  • Proven track record working with the US market and understanding US business culture

As a plus

  • University graduate (preferably in Computer Sciences or a related tech field)
  • PMI/PMP certification
  • Working abroad experience

We offer

  • 40-hour workweek with flexible working hours (start from 8 to 11 am);
  • Annual paid vacations (18 working days);
  • Paid days off on National Holidays;
  • Paid sick leaves (10 working days);
  • Possibility to work remotely;
  • English classes (for different levels of language skills; etc.);
  • External and internal professional training and conferences;
  • Continuous learning opportunities and personal development plan;
  • Tax compensation (Social & Single Social Payment) and accounting support;
  • Engaging the working process with challenging tasks in a professional environment;
  • Open-minded, collaboration-friendly, invention-driven team;
  • Corporate and team events.

Responsibilities

  • Work with requirements from the customer’s side
  • Create project plans and estimations (pre-sale or during the project)
  • Make an analysis of possible risks and manage them
  • Leadership of the project team
  • Pay attention to resource planning
  • Always pay attention to the project’s pulse
  • Create workflows and documents

Project description


Asosa IT Solutions is a client-oriented innovative software development company with a passion for web and mobile application and complex server solutions development. Founded in 2014, its experienced and motivated team utilizes the latest technologies combined with marketing and user-oriented strategies to provide its clients with high-quality results. We craft mobile applications by combining strategy, design, development, testing, and deployment tools.
Asosa IT Solutions creates and strengthens the ability of their business to thrive towards employees’ goals as well as helping each one of them work together while feeling comfortable in a friendly working environment.
We welcome talented professionals to join our team!
