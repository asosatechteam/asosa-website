Continuous Support

We are responsible for the quality of all our products and aim for perfection. When the product is already released and published to the application markets, we offer a continuous support for app development to our clients.

Ongoing support is an integral component for cooperation with our startup customers who follow the Lean Startup approach to developing their business. The lean startup approach requires continuous iteration regarding a product based on the user feedback. That is why most of our clients need a responsive and agile team to be able to adapt the product quickly to a certain market fit.

Also, we ensure all our customers with three moths of free bug fixing after the launch of the product.
