Mobile app publishing

Mobile app publishing is an important process. Both for startups and business it can become a fundamental component of the successful release of the application.

At this stage experts of Asosa IT Solutions ensure that the representation of the app on the App Store or Google Play is of high quality and persuasive enough for the user to decide to download it.

/services/publishing/

To make sure that the application will stand out from the crowd and reach the end users after publishing we provide following steps:

  • Step 1

    Using app store optimization services

    To make sure that iOS or Android application will be approved by app stores and will get a support of these platforms, our experts follow all instructions and guidelines while developing an app.

  • Step 2

    Creating of all promotional materials.

    We provide mobile app publishing services with all necessary design, tutorials, and video advertising.s

  • Step 3

    Building billboard for app store submission services.

    We recommend our customers to find such name that will be broadly appealing for an as large amount of the audience as possible. Using keywords in the app description helps us to ensure search optimization.

  • Step 4

    Post-publishing support.

    After publishing on the App Store and Google Play, we provide our customers with updates and guarantee three months of free bug fixing.

  • Step 5

    Engaging and retaining users.

    We provide our customers with all necessary analytic tools that help to understand user behavior and give roadmap on how to change use cases to make the experience more involving.
